About TIRECS

CSP or Cyber Security Project is an app dedecated for testing networks and detecting faults.
DDoS security: DDoS stands for Distributed Denial for Service attack. In this digital attack, the attacker uses multiple numbers of devices to keep the web server engaged in accepting the requests sent by him from the multiple devices.
It creates fake website traffic on the server. To deal with this, Cybersecurity helps to provide a DDoS mitigation service to help cope with it which diverts the traffic to the other cloud-based servers and the situation gets resolved.
